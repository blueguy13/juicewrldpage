About Juice WRLD

Juice WRLD, born Jarad Anthony Higgins, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Known for his emotional lyrics and unique style, Juice WRLD left a lasting impact on the world through his music. From his breakout hit "Lucid Dreams" to his posthumous releases, his legacy continues to inspire millions worldwide.
